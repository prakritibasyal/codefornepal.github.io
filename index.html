About

We hope to help create a digitally and data empowered Nepal together where everyone can thrive. Here is our story so far.

  • 2014

    Our humble beginnings

    Founders Mia and Ravi organized their first digital literacy workshop for young women in Kathmandu and registered Code for Nepal, as a non-profit in the US.

  • 2015

    Responding to a historic crisis

    When the devastating earthquakes struck Nepal, Code for Nepal community members mobilized a digital response to help those in need and advocated for data-driven relief and recovery

  • 2016-2019

    Creating products and empowering aspiring tech professionals

    We launched open data and civic tech products, organized conferences, and hackathon, collaborated with partners such as Purdue University and gave scholarships to young women and men.
